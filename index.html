Emo-CampNet: Zero-shot Emotion Selectable End-to-End Text-based Speech Editing

Tao Wang (tao.wang@nlpr.ia.ac.cn)

National Laboratory of Pattern Recognition, CASIA, Beijing, China
CAS Center for Excellence in Brain Science and Intelligence Technology, Beijing, China
School of Artificial Intelligence, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China

Messages

In our previous work, we proposed the CampNet for end-to-end text-based speech editing, which can delete, replace and insert the speech at the word level by editing the transcription.

In this work, we propose Emo-CampNet, which can provide emotional options for generated speech on the basis of text-based speech editing, so as to make generated speech more expressive. This page shows the samples in the paper "Zero-shot Emotion Selectable End-to-End Text-based Speech Editing".

Experiments were based on VCTK and ESD . All test samples have not appeared in the training set and validation set.

Arxiv: https://arxiv.org/abs/2202.09950
